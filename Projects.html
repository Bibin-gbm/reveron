Brushing up your knowledge in Maths has never been easier. Learners India is an innovative revolutionary Digital School for Mathematics from Riveron. We personalized this unique project to create an ‘Ultimate Learning Experience’ for children to learn at their own pace. With a multitude of mathematical concepts, real world applications, synchronized 3D modelled videos and games, simplified self -study materials, analytical tests and interaction with experts, Learners India gives every child an excellent platform where they have all the information required to be a Master in Mathematics.
Learners India is not just only for students- It is as beneficial for teachers too. Maths anxiety is one of the most challenging task teachers have to overcome in the classroom and to remove this fear requires a lot of positive reinforcement. Our digital platform gives teachers new ways to engage students in the learning process. Parents can track and evaluate their children’s learning graph through a simplified dashboard in real time.
Finally, all the information you need to be a genius is at your fingertips- So come and be a member of our exclusive family.

Our Vision

To offer a personalized interactive learning platform for Mathematics with innovative 3D modelled video lectures along with other techniques to resemble the teacher-to- student direct learning through digital technology.

Our Mission

Inspire the students to overcome the fear of Maths and prepare them to be “future ready” as the proficiency in Mathematics is the main ingredient for success in walk of life.
