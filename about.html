About

E-learning is gaining a greater momentum compared to the conventional methods as the digitally delivered content directly to the learner offers a lot of convenience to him/her to learn at their pace and from the comfort of home. It also helps in mitigating the growing issues of traffic , crowded class rooms and searching for suitable home tuitions in the locality etc . Riveron Infotech Pvt Ltd has been set up to tap this opportunity and make the quality material available and affordable to all sections of the society. The company has registered office in Trivandrum, Kerala from which it operates its production, IT and Marketing functions. As a first step , under the brand of www.learnersindia.com, we offer curriculum based support for Mathematics for school students .Mathematics is a nightmare for many of them but unfortunately the control on Maths is essential for the success in other subjects too. Maths is all about practice - the more you practice, the better you gain control over it. We offer an excellent platform for students to practice in several ways to make them comfortable and master on it. We have utilized simple language ,animated graphics and real videos etc to make the learning an interesting one and creates a feel of a teacher with you always which resemble a one to one support.

Mission Statement

Inspire the students to overcome the fear of Maths and prepare them to be “future ready” as the proficiency in Mathematics is the main ingredient for success in walk of life
